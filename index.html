The Journey

Session 1

Crash Fistfight, Solaris, Elte, Jip Bip, and Kenra Whisper all met in a tavern outside Waterdeep and were petitioned by the innkeeper to find her son (maybe? need confirmation from Meri I can't remember). The party headed off towards Baldur's Gate, following the inkeeper sons (?) trail.
On their way they find themselves surrounded my a mist, eventually wandering into the lands of Barovia.

The Party travels to the Village of Barovia where they don't see anyone, eventually being lured into Death House. While in there (stuff happens, fill this all out later. I'm lazy). Crash Fistfight dies from suplexing a grick.

Session 2

Still in Death House, Solaris loses his life heroically holding off a Shambling Mound so the rest of The Party can escape. Kenra Whisper is de-footed during the escape. After finally escaping, what's left of The Party meet Varyn Grimlance, Ffion, and Cipher Dupe, inviting them on their journey.

Session 3

The Party set out to find a way to re-attach Kenra Whisper's foot, first heading to the local tavern, meeting Rotton and inviting him to join them. The party is directed to Bildrath Cantmir's shop, while there Rotton and him get in an argument, starting a century long feud between their families. Bildrath Cantmir eventually sells the party a concoction of gay juice that they proceed to pour on Kenra Whisper's wound, allowing them to re-attach his foot.

The Party is approached by Ismark Kolyana, asking for their help. He petitions them to take his sister, Ireena Kolyana, to Vallaki. He explains that Strahd von Zarovich is obsessed with her and wishes to kidnap her. The Party accepts the quest.

Session 4

The Party sees a 'march of the dead' or something, goes to the church to deliver the body of the father of Ireena Kolyana and Ismark Kolyana. The Party talks to the creepy priests, suggests we talk to the gypsies at the Tser Pool.

The Party heads to talk to the gypsies at the Tser Pool, the old woman there tells the party that we could take Ireena Kolyana to Krezk instead if Vallaki doesn't work out. There is serious sexual tension between the old woman and Rotton.

The Party returns to the Village of Barovia.

Session 5

In the Village of Barovia, The Party sees an old woman going around selling pastries(?). They find she also accepts children as payment (obvi tryna be a minecraft youtuber or something). Bjorn took the child back, shaking the bag like a fish tank, then deciding to free him, which made him run back to his father, Father Jarov. The Party decided to not scar the child anymore and went about their business. They picked up Ireena Kolyana and finally headed out west from the Village of Barovia.

On their way, Bjorn wasn't paying attention and steps in a bear trap. At almost the exact same time Varyn Grimlance disappears in a poof of smoke, now Pest standing in his place. Bjorn blames the small wizard for the bear trap, causing Pest to fling his bestfriend Spud at him. Eventually when everything calms down, The Party invites Pest to join them.

The Party eventually comes to a pack of starving wolves, after a long fight, The Party eventually decides to retreat to safety.|

Session 5

The Party wakes up after a long rest, the day beginning with Elte throwing a magic stone at Bjorn, hitting him in the eye and temporarily blinding him.

Back on the trail up to Vallaki, Bjorn, who is pulling the cart, begins to follow a raven down a side path, eventually finding a broken wagon, pools of blood, raven feathers, and one deep footprint. Eventually while investigating, the party hears footsteps approaching from behind, seeing a cloaked woman, Pest can tell she is the raven they followed here. She says a bunch of creepy stuff, eventually letting The Party know the footprint by the wagon is from her failing to land correctly. She also explains the wagon is from The Martikovs, down the path to the south-left. After being weird for a while, she leaves, saying "You didnt see me, you just heard a raven" (or something idk i have bad memory). Through investigating the wagon, The Party finds a bunch of wine bottles, all marked as from the Martikov Winery. Instead of investigating the Martikov Winery at this moment, The Party decided to focus on delivering Ireena Kolyana to her destination before making too many detours. The Party eventually makes their way back to the main road, continuing their journey to Vallaki.
